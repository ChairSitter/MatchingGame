Rules:

It's is a race against the computer to 100 pts!

You have 4 boxes, and so does the computer.

Clicking "PLAY" starts a round. Each round, boxes are randomly assigned a background color and text color.

The side with the most valuable scoring combination receives the corresponding points each round. Read about the scoring combinations below and reference the cheat sheet during the game.

The "MULTIPLY" button lets you bet on the outcome to win points and beat the computer. Multiply to boost the score when you win, but be careful not to boost the computer's when you lose!

The Multiply Button:

The multiply button adds a betting challenge to the game. When the round starts, it will count down from x5 to x2 as more boxes are shown.
If you click it, any points scored during the round by you or the computer will be multiplied by the number shown on the button.
If you win the round this will boost your score, but be careful- if you lose this will boost the computer's score instead!
Click earlier to guess the outcome and place a big, risky bet on your side to win the round. Click later to utilize your comparison skills and score some sure-shot points... unless you're wrong!

Tiebreaker (1 pt):
A tie can occur if both sides have the same scoring combination or the same number of matches.
In case of a tie, the most common color on each side is compared, and the one with more occurrences wins 1 pt.
If still tied, the second-most common colors are compared, and so on.
If a tie remains, The side with the most warm colors (red, orange, yellow) wins 1 pt.
After that, it's just a tie.
