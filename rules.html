Rules:

  • Press "Play" and hope for luck in this race to 50 pts!
  • You have 4 boxes and so does the computer.
  • Each round, boxes are assigned a random background color and text color.
  • The side with the single most valuable scoring combination receives the corresponding point value each round.
  • Use the multiply button to boost your score, but be careful not to boost the computer's score!

The Multiply Button:

The multiply button adds a betting challenge to the game.
If you click it, any points scored during the round will be multiplied by the number shown on the button.
If you win the round this will boost your score, but be careful- if you lose you will boost the computer's score instead!

One more thing: if you multiply and the round ends in a tie, the computer gets 1 point. Click carefully to avoid this penalty!
