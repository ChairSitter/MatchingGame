Rules:

  • Press "Play" and hope for good luck!
  • Score more points than the computer by matching colors and making scoring combinations.
  • You have 4 boxes and so does the computer.
  • Each round, boxes are assigned a random background color and text color.
  • The side with the most valuable scoring combination receives the corresponding point value at the end of the round.
  • Boost your score by using the multiply button wisely, but be careful!

Multiply Button:


Pressing Play activates the multiply button.
Its value will count down from 5 to 2, and it will disable between rounds.
If you click the multiply button, the round's point payout will be multiplied by the current value shown on the button.
If used wisely you can boost your score, but be careful or you might boost the computer's score instead!
